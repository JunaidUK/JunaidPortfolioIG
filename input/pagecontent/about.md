# About Junaid Siddiqui

I am a health technology professional with deep expertise in HL7 FHIR and a track record of delivering interoperability solutions across the payer, provider, and health IT vendor ecosystem. My work sits at the intersection of clinical workflows, regulatory compliance, and modern software engineering.

---

## Professional Background

Over the past 7+ years I have delivered healthcare technology projects at Vermonster across greenfield startups and Fortune 10 enterprises, spanning the US and UK. My engagements have ranged from FHIR-based EHR integrations and state-level interoperability initiatives to AI-enabled clinical tooling, with a consistent focus on translating complex technical requirements into production software that holds up in real clinical environments.

My FHIR practice covers the full implementation lifecycle:

- **Implementation Guide Authoring** — Designing and publishing FHIR IGs using FSH/SUSHI and the HL7 IG Publisher, conformant with US Core, Da Vinci, CARIN, and other nationally recognized profiles
- **FHIR Server Implementation** — Building and configuring FHIR-compliant APIs (HAPI FHIR, Azure Health Data Services, AWS HealthLake, Google Cloud Healthcare API)
- **Data Mapping & Terminology** — Translating legacy data models (HL7 v2, CDA/C-CDA, proprietary formats) to FHIR R4, including code system mapping with SNOMED CT, LOINC, RxNorm, and ICD-10
- **SMART on FHIR** — Integrating SMART App Launch flows and OAuth 2.0 authorization for EHR-connected applications
- **Standards Advisory** — Supporting organizations through CMS and ONC regulatory requirements (Interoperability Rule, TEFCA, Prior Authorization)
